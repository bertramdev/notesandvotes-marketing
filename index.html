Frequently Asked Questions

Quickly & easily answer potential customer questions to protect brand reputation and increase sales

 

Manage Your Brand

  • When is Notes and Votes the right solution?

    Notes and Votes can provide a full featured solution for most user-generated-content features found in e-commerce and marketing web sites. These include product reviews, community Q&A, and user forums. Notes and Votes is particularly relevant for existing web properties that lack these features. Notes and Votes may also be the right solution for prototyping initiatives and validating hypotheses that involve user-generated content.

  • When is Notes and Votes NOT the right solution?

    If you are building a new e-commerce or CMS-based website, you may be able to find an e-commerce or CMS platform (such as Magento, Drupal, or Joomla) or platform plug-in that offers many of the same features provided by Notes and Votes. Notes and Votes may not be the right solution if you are looking for built-in ERP integration, integrated email marketing, or advanced reporting options. Also, Notes and Votes does not currently support image, video or document uploads directly, although mash-up integrations with other providers are fairly straight-forward using the Notes and Votes API.

  • How much effort does it take to implement product reviews using Notes and Votes?

    The simplest implementation can be completed in less than an hour. Just sign up for a Notes and Votes account and paste the appropriate widget Javascript into your product detail page. A fully customized implementation with an advanced authorization scheme might take anywhere from several days to several weeks to complete, depending on whether the implementation can leverage our existing javascript widget library.

  • Will Notes and Votes impact the performance of my web site?

    Notes and Votes functionality is typically executed by the web browser and activated after the initial page load. Therefore your web site server performance will not be impacted and page load times will be only be negligibly impacted during the initial download and caching of the Notes and Votes widget javascript. However, the runtime performance of Notes and Votes widgets in the web browser may vary depending on the user's computer and network bandwidth.

  • What happens to my site when the Notes and Votes system is unavailable?

    Because Notes and Votes functionality is typically activated after the initial page load, your web site availability is not impacted by a Notes and Votes system outage. Also, Notes and Votes widgets degrade gracefully if the Notes and Votes system is unavailable. The widgets can be configured to be  hidden themselves or display a custom user message.

  • Can I control what user submissions are visible on my web site?

    You can optionally turn on the "approvals" feature for your Notes and Votes account. When this feature is activated, user submissions are staged and require approval in the Administrative Web Console or via the API before they are visible on your site. Also, user submissions can be deactivated at any time via the Administrative Web Console or API.

  • Can I implement a custom authorization scheme for user submissions?

    Notes and Votes offers a number of authorization schemes with every account, including API Keys, expiring tokens, and network restrictions. The Notes and Votes widgets can be configured to communicate directly with the Notes and Vote server or to proxy endpoint that you control. Using straight-forward strategies, you can limit user submissions to authenticated users, confirmed buyers, or any other classification that fits your needs.

  • How does Notes and Votes impact SEO?

    Notes and Votes can boost the SEO quality of your pages through the use of meta data that is automatically embedded by our widgets.

  • Can I export user submissions out of Notes and Votes if I choose to use a different solution in the future?

    User submissions are fully portable. User submissions can be exported using the Notes and Votes API, or with the help of customer service.
